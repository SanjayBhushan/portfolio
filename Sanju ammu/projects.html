PROJECT 1                                    

PROJECT NAME: "Smart Hydroponics Farming System"

AIM: The Smart Hydroponics Farming System is an innovative and sustainable approach to modern agriculture that leverages technology to cultivate plants without soil, using nutrient-rich water solutions. This project aims to address traditional farming challenges such as water scarcity, land limitations, and unpredictable weather conditions by creating an automated and controlled environment for optimal  plant growth.
